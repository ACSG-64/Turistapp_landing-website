What is Turistapp?

Turistapp is the perfect application for travelers and adventurers of any kind who want freedom and flexibility. Just choose a tour from the many available around the world and start your adventure at your own pace and without pressure. You will not only discover interesting places but also learn from them to get the most out of each visit.
No more need to follow a set schedule on conventional tours, now an amazing sightseeing experience your way thanks to Turistapp is waiting to be installed on your Android!

Turistapp features

List of locations and progress tracking.

See all the places included in the tour, organize them by their distance from you, and keep track of the places you have already visited.

Geofencing and interactive map.

When you are close to a tour location, Turistapp will let you know by sending you a notification. You will also be able to see all the locations of the places on the map.

Details and information.

Discover interesting and relevant information about the place where you are, plus you can have the information read aloud to you for your convenience.
